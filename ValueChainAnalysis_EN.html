📋 Calculation Structure
① RSP Ex.VAT = Consumer Price (RSP) ÷ 1.1 (VAT excluded)
② Supply Price = RSP Ex.VAT × (1 − GP%)  —  GP = Retail Margin basis
③ TAC = Landed Cost + Packaging + Labor + Logistics(Supply%) + Other + Vendor Fee(Supply%)
④ Revenue = Supply Price − TAC  —  Gross Margin = Revenue ÷ Supply Price
⑤ Trade Conditions shown separately (excluded from GP, reference cost only)

📌 Promotion Profitability Calculation
• Promo Supply = Normal Supply × (1 − Discount × Our Burden Rate)
• Promo TAC = Landed Cost + Packaging + Labor + (Logistics% × Promo Supply) + Other + (Vendor Fee% × Promo Supply)
• Promo GM = (Promo Supply − Promo TAC) ÷ Promo Supply
• Results show both Normal Sale and Promo Sale scenarios side by side
